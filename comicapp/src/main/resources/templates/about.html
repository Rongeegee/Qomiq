About

Explore:

Want to view your favorite comics? Use the explore page to search for your favorite comics, series, or authors based on the their name and category. The search results can also be sorted by date or view count.


Create:

Our create page provides you a canvas with many functionalities to make your comic. Upon finishing, save it or publish it to our website.


View:

Iterate over pages and comics; comment on or like/disike a comic.


Telephone Game:

Create your own game or participate on a game created by one of the author's you are subscribed to. Games work by someone submitting the first page of a comic, and then submissions are voted on each round. The submissions with most votes will be displayed after the game is finalized.
